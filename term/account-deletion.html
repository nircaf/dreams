Account Deletion

Delete Your HypnoDreamz Account

HypnoDreamz, also referred to as DreamzBLE in the app and device software, lets users request deletion of their app account and associated app data.

This public page can be used as the Delete account URL in Google Play Console: https://hypnodreamz.com/term/account-deletion.html.

01

Request Account Deletion In The App

  1. Open HypnoDreamz.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Find the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.
02

Request Account Deletion Without The App

Email support@hypnodreamz.com from the email address associated with your HypnoDreamz account and include "HypnoDreamz account deletion" in the subject.

If you used Google sign-in, include the Google email address used for the app. We may ask for additional information only as needed to verify that you control the account.

Email Deletion Request
03

What Is Deleted

We delete the app account record and associated HypnoDreamz cloud data linked to the account, including:

  • Session history
  • Questionnaire logs
  • Device session logs
  • Stimulation threshold records
  • Daily Pulse entries
  • LSense samples
  • App diagnostic logs linked to the account
04

What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, warranty, dispute-resolution, or regulatory purposes. Retained data is limited to what is necessary for those purposes.

05

Deletion And Retention Period

In-app deletion is processed immediately when successful. Email deletion requests are reviewed and processed within 30 days after we verify the account owner.

Backup copies and operational logs may take up to 30 additional days to expire from routine backup systems. Records that must be retained for legal, security, warranty, dispute-resolution, or regulatory reasons are retained only for the period required for that purpose and then deleted or de-identified.
