Product Description & Intended Use

Dreamz is a consumer wellness wearable that uses non-invasive biometric sensing (including electroencephalography-inspired surface electrode technology) and adaptive low-intensity electrical stimulation to monitor and support improved sleep patterns in healthy adults. The Dreamz sleep mask is designed to:

  • Detect and interpret surface-level brainwave activity during sleep;
  • Deliver gentle, precisely timed electrical stimulation signals to support the user's transition into deeper sleep stages;
  • Collect and synchronize sleep-related biometric data to a companion mobile application.

Dreamz is a consumer wellness product, not a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition, including but not limited to insomnia, sleep apnea, narcolepsy, epilepsy, or any neurological or psychiatric disorder. References to percentage improvements in sleep onset, sleep quality, or daily wellness reflect user-reported survey results and are not guaranteed outcomes.

Product specifications, features, and availability are subject to change at Dreamz's sole discretion. Final product specifications will be confirmed at the time of order fulfillment.
